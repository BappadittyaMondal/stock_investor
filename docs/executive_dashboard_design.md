# HFOS v5.0 — Executive Dashboard Design

## Target Audience
Designed for Portfolio Managers (PMs), Founders, and Family Office heads who require oversight without getting bogged down in individual ticker analysis.

## Key Metrics Displayed
- Total AUM
- Active Deployed Capital vs Cash Reserves
- Alpha Generated vs Benchmark (Nifty 500)

## The AI Executive Briefing
Instead of forcing the executive to parse charts, the `AICopilot` generates a daily natural-language briefing. It answers:
- What is the market regime?
- Is the portfolio stable?
- Where are the opportunities?
- What are the immediate macro/policy risks?
- What action should be taken today?

## Risk Panel
A consolidated risk panel immediately exposes Portfolio Beta, Max Sector Concentration, and the presence of any regulatory red flags (ASM/GSM). This ensures institutional compliance is always visible to leadership.
